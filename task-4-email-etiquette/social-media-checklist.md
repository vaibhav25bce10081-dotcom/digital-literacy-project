# Responsible Social Media Checklist for College Students

## Do's
1. **Set Profiles to Private:** Regularly review and update privacy settings on all platforms to control who sees your content.
2. **Think Before You Post:** Consider how a future employer or professor would view your content before hitting "send."
3. **Build a Professional Brand:** Use platforms like LinkedIn to showcase your projects, skills, and academic achievements.
4. **Fact-Check Information:** Verify the accuracy of news or academic information before sharing it to prevent the spread of misinformation.
5. **Engage Positively:** Use social media to participate in constructive discussions and network with industry professionals.

## Don'ts
1. **Post Unprofessional Content:** Avoid sharing photos or text that depict illegal activities, offensive behavior, or excessive partying.
2. **Vent About Academics or Work:** Never disparage professors, colleagues, or employers online; digital footprints are permanent.
3. **Engage in Cyberbullying:** Refrain from participating in heated online arguments or posting derogatory comments about others.
4. **Overshare Personal Information:** Protect your safety by not posting your current location, home address, or private contact details.
5. **Ignore Privacy Policies:** Don't skip reading the terms of service; understand how your data is being used and stored by different apps.
